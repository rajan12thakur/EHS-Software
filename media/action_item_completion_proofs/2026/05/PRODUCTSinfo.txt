
what is BSR 360 SOFTWARE AND WHAT ARE ITS 9 PRINCIPLES

Protegk IT’s BSR 360 is a software platform designed for BRSR (Business Responsibility and Sustainability Reporting) and ESG compliance in India. It helps companies collect, manage, and report sustainability data in line with SEBI’s BRSR requirements. The software includes dashboards, audit trails, ESG KPI tracking, task management, and automated report generation. 

The “9 principles” associated with BSR 360 are actually the 9 BRSR principles based on India’s National Guidelines on Responsible Business Conduct (NGRBC). These principles guide how companies should operate responsibly and sustainably. 

The 9 BRSR Principles
Ethical, Transparent & Accountable Governance
Businesses should operate honestly, ethically, and with accountability.

Sustainable & Safe Products/Services
Companies should provide products and services that are environmentally sustainable and safe for consumers.

Employee Well-being
Businesses should ensure fair treatment, safety, development, and welfare of employees.

Stakeholder Responsiveness
Companies should respect and respond to the interests of stakeholders such as customers, suppliers, investors, and communities.

Human Rights
Businesses should respect and promote human rights across operations and supply chains.

Environmental Protection
Companies should protect and restore the environment by reducing pollution, emissions, and waste.

Responsible Public Policy Engagement
Businesses should engage with government and policy-making transparently and responsibly.

Inclusive Growth & Equitable Development
Companies should support social and economic development for all sections of society.

Responsible Consumer Engagement
Businesses should provide value to consumers responsibly through ethical marketing, transparency, and quality service. 

In simple terms, BSR 360 is a compliance and ESG reporting tool that helps companies track and report performance against these 9 sustainability principles.
